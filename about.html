About Kajiado East

Kajiado East Constituency

Kajiado East Constituency is a vibrant and growing community, rich in Maasai culture and heritage. Located in Kajiado County, the constituency plays a crucial role in regional development through agriculture, trade, and education. With a focus on sustainability, infrastructure, and empowerment of local communities, Kajiado East aims to create opportunities for all its residents, especially the youth and women.

About Current MP

About Hamisi

Hon. Maimai Hamisi Kakuta is the current Member of Parliament for Kajiado East. He is dedicated to fostering community development, focusing on empowering local businesses, improving education, and enhancing infrastructure. His leadership is centered on uplifting the youth, women, and marginalized groups in the constituency.

Youth and Women Empowerment Programs

Hon. Hamisi Maimai Kakuta has made significant contributions to his community and constituency through his leadership and development-oriented initiatives. A native of Kajiado County, Hamisi Kakuta began his political journey driven by a passion for social and economic advancement. His rise to political prominence started with his grassroots involvement in addressing issues that affected marginalized communities, especially focusing on improving education, healthcare, and infrastructure.

As a Member of Parliament for Kajiado East, Hon. Kakuta has spearheaded numerous development projects aimed at uplifting the lives of his constituents. One of his key accomplishments has been in the education sector, where he has consistently worked to improve school infrastructure, provide scholarships for needy students, and support the empowerment of youth through educational opportunities. His commitment to education extends beyond just infrastructure, as he also advocates for policies that ensure equal access to quality learning for all.

In the healthcare sector, Hon. Kakuta has made significant strides by improving access to medical services, particularly in rural and underserved areas of Kajiado East. He has facilitated the construction and upgrading of health facilities, ensuring that residents have access to essential healthcare services. Additionally, he has supported various health programs focused on maternal health, immunization, and combating common diseases in the region.

Hon. Kakuta's focus on infrastructure development is also noteworthy. Under his leadership, the constituency has seen improved road networks, water supply systems, and electrification projects. These initiatives have not only enhanced the quality of life for the residents but also boosted economic activities by improving access to markets and essential services.

His approach to development is characterized by inclusivity, ensuring that all groups, including women, youth, and marginalized communities, have a voice in decision-making processes. Hon. Kakuta continues to work toward creating more opportunities for his constituents through economic empowerment initiatives, such as supporting small businesses and encouraging entrepreneurship.

Beyond his local contributions, Hon. Hamisi Kakuta remains a dedicated servant leader with a vision for a prosperous and united Kajiado East, advocating for policies and projects that drive sustainable development and social equity.
